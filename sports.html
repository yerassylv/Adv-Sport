Paragliding

Recommended Age: 18-60 years
Physical Requirements: Good overall health, no severe heart or respiratory issues.
Location: Tien Shan
Duration: 20-30 minutes per flight
Why Choose Paragliding?
Paragliding offers an unmatched bird's-eye view of stunning landscapes, making it an ideal choice for thrill-seekers.

Skiing

Recommended Age: 5 years and above
Physical Requirements: Basic physical fitness, no severe joint or back problems.
Location: Shymbulak
Season: December to March
Why Choose Skiing?
Skiing combines adrenaline with the beauty of snowy slopes. It's perfect for those who enjoy both speed and nature.

Skydiving

Recommended Age: 18-50 years
Physical Requirements: Weight limit of 90-100 kg, no severe heart issues.
Location: Astana
Duration: 5-7 minutes freefall, total 20 minutes with preparation
Why Choose Skydiving?
Skydiving is the ultimate thrill, offering a unique perspective of the world while testing your limits and courage.
