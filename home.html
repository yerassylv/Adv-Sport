Who Should Avoid These Activities?

Adventure sports are thrilling but not suitable for everyone. If you have any of the following conditions, it is strongly advised to avoid participating:

  • Severe heart conditions or cardiovascular diseases
  • Uncontrolled high blood pressure
  • Epilepsy or history of seizures
  • Pregnancy
  • Recent surgeries or severe injuries
  • Severe fear of heights or anxiety disorders
